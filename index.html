scrambled eggs

Egg are one of my favourite food. here is a recepie for deliciously rich scrambled egg

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1tbs Butter
  • 2tbs Cream

Method

  1. MELT BUTTER IN A FRYING PEN OVER A MEDIUM HEAT
  2. GENTLY MIX THE EGG AND CREAMIN A BOWL
  3. ONCE BUTTER HAS MELTED & CREAM & EGGS
  4. USING A SPATULA FOLDING THE EGGS FROM THE EDGE OF THE PAN TO THE CENTER EVERY 20 SECONDS (AS IF YOU ARE MAKING AN OMELETT)
  5. WHEN THE EGGS ARE STILL MOIST REMOVE FROM THE HEAT (IT WILL CONTINUE TO COOK OMTHE PLATE UNTIL SERVED)
